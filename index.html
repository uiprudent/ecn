About ECN

Leading Nigeria's Energy Sector Development

The Energy Commission of Nigeria (ECN) is the apex energy institution in Nigeria, established to advise the Federal Government on national energy policies and coordinate energy-related activities across all sectors.

We are committed to ensuring sustainable energy development and security for Nigeria through strategic planning, policy formulation, and coordinated implementation of energy programs.
