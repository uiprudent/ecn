General Services Department

The General Services Department provides essential administrative support services, facility management, and logistics coordination to ensure smooth operations across the Energy Commission of Nigeria.

Functions of the Department

Transport Fleet Management

Management of the transport fleet of the Commission to ensure efficient mobility for staff and operations.

Fleet Maintenance

Ensure prompt and value-for-money maintenance of the Commission's transport fleet.

Facility Management

Be responsible for facility management of the Commission's headquarters, ensuring optimal working conditions.

Store Management

Manage the store at the Commission's headquarters, ensuring proper inventory control and supply management.
